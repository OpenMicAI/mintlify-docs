## What is Openmic?

Openmic is a comprehensive platform for building voice-powered AI applications. We handle the complex infrastructure so you can focus on creating exceptional conversational experiences.

<Note>
	**Perfect for:** Customer support automation, sales calls, appointment
	booking, lead qualification, and any scenario requiring natural voice
	interactions.
</Note>

### Key Capabilities

Openmic enables you to:

-   **Engage naturally** with callers through human-like conversations
-   **Handle calls at scale** with inbound and outbound telephony
-   **Integrate seamlessly** with your existing tools and databases
-   **Automate complex workflows** like booking, support, and sales processes

## How Voice AI Works

Every Openmic system combines three core technologies working together:
