OpenMic.ai enables teams to quickly build smart voice assistants that sound natural and human-like. Our platform simplifies the complexities of developing voice AI applications, such as choosing and maintaining infrastructure for LLMS, TTS, and ASR. This allows developers to focus on their core business logic and deliver results in a matter of days, rather than months.
